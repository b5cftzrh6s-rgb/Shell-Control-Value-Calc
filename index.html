🎯 Revenue Claim Model — Controls how recovered good parts are valued (prevents double counting)

Model B formula (from Shell Control Wetting In Doc): Revenue Uplift = V × R × P × (1 − VC%) where V = annual attempted castings, R = scrap-rate reduction, P = avg selling price, VC% = variable cost of price (40–60% typical).

1 Scrap Reduction from Shell Consistency

Recovered production cost from fewer shell-related defects (cracking, weight deviation, bulge, pinholes) once real-time viscosity monitoring stabilizes the slurry inside its control band.

savings = recovered_parts × cost_per_part
